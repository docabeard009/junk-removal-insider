What Haulers Will Take in Washington

Standard junk removal in Washington covers furniture, mattresses, appliances, electronics, yard waste, general household clutter, and light construction debris. Items requiring special handling under NJ DEP rules — including oil-based paint, propane tanks, fluorescent bulbs, and car batteries — cannot go in a standard junk truck and must be disposed of through Warren County's HHW program.
